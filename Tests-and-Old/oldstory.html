About


Hello. This website is a part of Reese's industrial design graduate thesis project in 2023-2024.


The project intends to design a set of usable furniture 'creatures' that express themselves through anthropomorphic elements such as 'facial' features, 'body' gestures and movements. The furniture 'creatures' have their own personality, preferences, lives and they can interact with each other and the user and tell their stories in some way.


Through the design of the furniture 'creatures', I want to encourage people to observe all kinds of noticeable 'fun' and 'happy incidents' happening in daily lives, slow them down and step out of their struggles. I want to invite users to rethink about themselves, their surroundings, others in the same environment, the relationship between them, and to be more humble, responsible and caring for their objects.


This website serves as a never-finished, continuous, always-updatable archive of the owner's journey with the furnicreatures.
