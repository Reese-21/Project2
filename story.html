Furnicreature

Furnicreature refers to a kingdom of inorganic creatures that cleverly blend into domestic environments as furniture, a concealed truth seldom acknowledged until recently.

This mysterious yet amiable kingdom of creatures has only recently come to the public's attention, with the possibility of further species discoveries looming on the horizon.

Target Users

  • Families with children
  • Young people who live alone and want companion (18-32)
  • Dormitory/ Shared houses with friendly roommates
  • People who want to keep pets but cannot
  • People fond of the kawaii culture
  • People who anthropomorphize--people who are curious/ imaginative/ lonely

Their Roles

  • Domestic companion and helpern
  • Conversation starter
  • Educate children; advocate care for objects
  • Create happy moments in life

Family Tree

The Furnicreature kingdom is structured into three phylum, seven orders, and 19 species, including newly discovered species. These creatures thrive within domestic environments, exhibiting diverse forms and lifestyles suited to various habitats.
